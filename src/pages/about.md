## Hey, I'm Autumn! 👋

Software engineer, podcast co-host, conference speaker, mom of three little boys, and powered entirely by coffee, books and tacos. ☕ 🌮

### 💻 What I Do

I'm currently a Software Engineer at **Microsoft Azure Linux**, where I specialize in Linux, Security, Data and open-source. Previously, I was a **Product Manager for Azure Linux** with a focus on Security. Before Microsoft, I spent time at **Amazon Web Services** — first as a **NoSQL Database Solutions Architect**, Helping organizations gain business value by helping them come up with the right purpose build No SQL database for their use case. I also use Python and Java to write sample code and build solutions to help unblock customers and then as a **Software Engineer** on **Amazon Corretto (Java)** contibuting to the Opensource community. Ive been features in news articles on CNBC and nominated for the Snohomish County Emerging Leadrs Award.

I'm a proud member of **Milspouse coders**, I sit on the  Board as the Education board chair*, leading the chapter in the Greater Seattle Area. As a military spouse, I know firsthand how important community, flexibility, and advocacy are for getting into tech. Ive sat on the board of Directors for the Snohomish County United way, I am a Proud alumni of **"Rewriting the Code" alumni**. I love to volunteer and mentor women, coaching hackathons, and working with students helping them get into technology. Ive recently founded a non profit called little guild to help address food insecurities and need in our communities.

#### Some of my talks:

**🤖 The Intersectionality of Human Psychology, Security and The Era of AI and Misinformation** — If cybercrime were measured as a country, it would be the world's third-largest economy. On average, companies experience 21–24 days of downtime after a ransomware attack. If a Hong Kong bank can be convinced to wire transfer $35 million by a deepfake, how do we protect grandma? This talk explores how we educate and safeguard the future as technology advances faster than we can keep up.

**👗 Barbie's Journey: A CI/CD Tale of Transformation** — The Barbie movie serves as a compelling analogy for understanding CI/CD in software development. Through a structured analysis of key plot points, attendees gain a deeper understanding of CI/CD concepts and their practical applications in real-world scenarios.

**👩‍💻 Empowering Women in Data: Shaping an Inclusive AI Future** — Exploring the vital role of women in shaping the future of data and its impact on AI. Uncovering the transformative potential of accessible cloud computing for women in tech and the urgency of fostering diversity in data to combat bias and ensure an equitable AI future.

**🔍 Hidden in Plain Sight: Addressing Data Bias in AI-Driven Systems** — Examining how biased datasets propagate through machine learning pipelines and influence outcomes at scale. From data labeling and feature selection to model optimization — even small biases can magnify under automation. Covering frameworks for bias detection, debiasing techniques, and evaluation methodologies to build more fair and accountable AI systems.
